# Scathat Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 13, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Scathat ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our browser extension and related services.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Wallet Addresses**: We collect wallet addresses you connect to analyze transaction security
- **Smart Contract Data**: Contract addresses and code you choose to scan
- **Extension Settings**: Your preferences for security scanning and notifications

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Transaction Data**: Public blockchain data related to transactions you analyze
- **Usage Analytics**: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
- **Technical Information**: Browser type, extension version, and device information

### 2.3 Blockchain Data
We access public blockchain data through:
- Ethereum Mainnet
- Polygon Network  
- Base Network
- Optimism Network
- Arbitrum Network
- Core Network
- Other supported blockchain networks

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide real-time smart contract security analysis
- Detect and prevent potential threats and vulnerabilities
- Improve and optimize our extension functionality
- Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
- Develop new security features and services

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage
- **Local Storage**: Most data is stored locally in your browser
- **Temporary Processing**: Contract analysis data is processed temporarily
- **No Personal Data**: We do not store personally identifiable information

### 4.2 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data transmissions are encrypted
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls to protect your information
- **Regular Audits**: Security audits and vulnerability assessments

## 5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including:
- **Blockchain Nodes**: For accessing blockchain data
- **Analytics Services**: For understanding usage patterns (anonymous)
- **Cloud Services**: For processing intensive security analysis

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
- You can disconnect wallets at any time
- You can clear extension data through browser settings
- You can disable the extension when not in use

### 6.2 Data Retention
- Wallet addresses: Until you disconnect
- Scan results: Temporary processing only
- Analytics: Anonymous and aggregated

## 7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
